About Cyrus

Built with purpose — for those who steward their finances for something greater.

"But as for me and my house, we will serve the LORD."

Our Mission

Cyrus exists to help everyday people take confident, clear-headed control of their finances. We believe financial freedom is not an end in itself — it is a means to live generously, serve others, and honor God. Every feature in this application was designed with that purpose in mind: to remove friction, reduce anxiety, and give you more time and resources to invest in what matters most.

Stewardship

We are not owners of what we have been given — we are managers. Cyrus is built on the conviction that wise stewardship of wealth creates ripple effects far beyond the individual: families are supported, communities are strengthened, and the Kingdom is advanced when God's people manage money with discipline and intention.

Generosity First

Growing your portfolio is not the goal — it is the tool. We hope that every gain tracked in this application leads to a question: "How can I use this for others?" Whether that means giving to your church, supporting a mission, or providing for your family, Cyrus is here to help you get there faster.

What We Stand For

  • Privacy

    Your financial data never leaves your device. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud.

  • Automation

    Your time is valuable. Cyrus handles the repetitive work so you can focus on living.

  • Integrity

    We build software we would trust with our own accounts — transparent, honest, and open.

  • Purpose

    Profit without purpose is empty. We build tools for people who want their wealth to mean something.

Whatever exchange you use, whatever asset you hold — may your financial decisions reflect the same bold declaration Joshua made to his household. Choose whom you will serve, and serve with everything you have.
